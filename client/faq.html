Frequently Asked Questions

Why are you called American Dominican Tours?

American Dominican Tours was started by the husband-and-wife duo of Whitney and Americo. Americo is Dominican and Whitney is American. They decided to start a business to bring their love for travel to everyone! They thought the best name for the business was one that represented both of their countries.

Are your tours safe?

Our clients' safety and happiness are our top priorities. All tour providers are heavily vetted and meet our high standards of safety and quality.

What makes you different from other companies?

The owners of American Dominican Tours have worked in tourism for decades. They have met hundreds of tourists and made their vacations magical! The connections they have with local tour providers guarantees a great time for all their clients.
